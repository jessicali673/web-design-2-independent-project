Theatre Poster

A poster design made for The Blacksheep Theatre's It Was Nice to Have Giraffes written and directed by Dave Dawson. The story is about loss and the importance of appreciating what we already have. Created using Adobe Illustrator, this poster uses the illustration, type, and colour in a dynamic way that showcases the play. The creative process from brainstorming and sketching to final production has been exciting all around!

Imagery & Typography Spread

This spread showcases a personal photography and how it works together with the type, taking into consideration the hierarchy and readability while making the spread creative and engaging. Image editing is done using Photoshop and the spread is created using InDesign.

Disappearing Bees Infographic

Disappearing Bees is an infographic, showcasing my ability to fit all content on a single 11″ by 17″ page that is both informative and with custom graphic visuals. All illustrations, colours and iconagraphy used in the infographic showcases a style that represents the topic well, keeping it relevent and engaging!
